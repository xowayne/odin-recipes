Air Fried Pork Belly Recipe

This pork belly recipe is a must in anyones repertoire! It is simple in nature but complex on the taste buds. If you're a pork belly lover like me, you'll definetly want to try this.

Ingredients

  • 1 kg pork belly sliced into thin strips
  • 3/4 cup of soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup of vinegar
  • 2 pcs Lime
  • 1 small bottle of sprite
  • 1 tsp ground pepper
  • 5 cloves of garlic
  • 1 tbsp of sugar
  • 2 tbsp of oyster sauce
  • 3 tbsp of ketchup
