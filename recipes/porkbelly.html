Pork Belly Recipe

Air Fried Pork Belly Recipe

This pork belly recipe is a must in anyones repertoire! It is simple in nature but complex on the taste buds. If you're a pork belly lover like me, you'll definetly want to try this.

Ingredients

  • 1 kg pork belly sliced into thin strips
  • 3/4 cup of soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup of vinegar
  • 2 pcs Lime
  • 1 small bottle of sprite
  • 1 tsp ground pepper
  • 5 cloves of garlic
  • 1 tbsp of sugar
  • 2 tbsp of oyster sauce
  • 3 tbsp of ketchup

Steps

  1. In large bowl, place all of the ingredients (for the sprite add a splash)
  2. Mix and let the pork belly marinate for at least 5 hrs
  3. Pre-heat air fryer to 395 degrees fahrenheit
  4. Place pork belly in air fryer and set for 10 mins
  5. flip at 5 minute mark (if pork belly was not marinated long enough, glaze pork with marinate before and after flip.)

Enjoy!
