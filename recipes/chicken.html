Chicken Breast Recipe

Garlic Butter Chicken Breast

This is one i've had the luxury of trying a few times. I would definetly consider it a a delicious dish when needing to whip up something quick! If you're a big fan of garlic based recipes, you'll love this one!

Note that this isnt a recipe of my own and therefore, id like to give credit to SF Cooking Studio on youtube. The link to their video is here:

Video Tutorial

Ingredients

  • Boneless chicken 300g
  • Black pepper total 1tsp
  • Chilli Flakes ½tsp
  • Salt
  • Oil ⅓cup
  • Unsalted Butter 30g
  • Chicken broth ½cup
  • Italian seasoning ½tsp
  • minced Garlic (3 cloves)
  • All purpose flour 3tsp

Steps

  1. Cut the breast chicken into bite size pieces
  2. Add a fair amount of salt, ½tsp black pepper, 2tsp All purpose flour to the chicken and mix
  3. Add cooking oil to the pan and preheat to medium-high
  4. Add the chicken to the pan and cook until golden brown
  5. Remove chicken from the pan. Add the unsalted butter and minced garlic
  6. Fry for 30 seconds. Then add 1tsp All purpose flour, the chicken broth and the chicken breast to the pan
  7. Sprinkle the chilli flakes, the italian seasoning and rest of the black pepper onto the chicken
  8. Stir for 2 minutes

Enjoy!
