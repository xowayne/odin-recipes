Medium Rare Pan Seared Steak Recipe

This is one of my personal favorite steak recipes of all time. A pan seared medium rare steak provides all the natural, juicy flavors expected of prime meat. We will be enhancing these tones by adding flavorful seasoning and a mouth watering, buttery sear leaving you speechless!

Ingredients

  • Prime Steak (New york or ribeye)
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Garlic powder
  • Butter (unsalted)
  • Thyme
  • Olive oil

Steps

  1. Coat room tempurature steak with salt, pepper and garlic powder
  2. Preheat pan (preferably cast iron) to Med-High heat and place olive oil in pan
  3. Place a modest amount of Olive oil in the pan
